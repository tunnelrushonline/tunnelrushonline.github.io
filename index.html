How to Play Tunnel Rush

Playing Tunnel Rush is easy to learn but hard to master. Move left or right to avoid red obstacles that may rotate or block your path. The further you go, the faster the game gets.

  • Use the left/right arrow keys or A/D keys to move
  • Press the spacebar to pause or restart

The game ends when you hit an obstacle. Your final distance is your score, so keep practicing to beat your personal best.

Game Modes

Classic Mode: Progress through increasingly difficult levels with more obstacles and faster speed.

2 Player Mode: Race against a friend on the same screen. The last player still running wins the match.

Tips & Tricks

  • Take a few runs to get used to the controls and speed
  • Focus on upcoming obstacles instead of reacting too late
